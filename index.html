Ideas & tips

How to choose the perfect wall art

A simple guide to help you pick the right design for your space — before you even book a call.

01

Start with the room's purpose

A puja room calls for serene deity art. A living room can be bold and vibrant. A restaurant should reflect its cuisine. Begin with what the space is for.

02

Choose colours that complement

Look at your existing furniture. Warm tones (terracotta, gold) suit traditional Indian decor. Cool tones (blue, green) work for modern minimalist spaces.

03

Pick the right wall

The best wall is usually the one you see first when entering — behind a sofa, headboard, or dining table. Avoid walls with too many windows or doors.

04

Get the size right

For an accent wall, the artwork should cover at least 60%. For statement pieces, aim for 40–50%. We'll help you measure and visualize before printing.

05

Mix tradition with modern

Pairing Madhubani or Warli motifs with minimalist furniture creates stunning contrast. Don't be afraid to blend heritage art with contemporary interiors.

06

Think long-term

Trends change, but classic art lasts. Choose designs that feel timeless to you, not just what's currently fashionable. Your walls should feel like home for years.
